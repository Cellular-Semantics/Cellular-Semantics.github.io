This is a static website for the Cellular Semantics team at the Wellcome Sanger Institute. It does not set advertising or analytics cookies and does not ask you for personal information.

The site is hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://docs.github.com/en/pages). Like any web host, GitHub's servers may record standard technical information (such as your IP address and browser type) when you access the site; this is handled under [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

Some pages link to external services (for example GitHub, the Ontology Lookup Service, CELLxGENE and ORCID). Those services have their own privacy policies.

If you have any questions, please contact us via our [GitHub organisation](https://github.com/Cellular-Semantics).
